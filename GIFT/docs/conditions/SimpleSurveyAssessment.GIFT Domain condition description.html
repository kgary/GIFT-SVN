Simple Survey Assessment Condition

This condition is normally used when the author wants to apply the scoring results of a survey to a concept and doesn't have another condition type that applies to the situation.  For example, suppose you would like to present a survey when the learner reaches a specific location in a virtual environment such as Unity.  You don't want the survey results to change the assessment for reaching that location but rather for another concept such as "identifying land features".  In this example you would want to use the simple survey assessment condition to apply the score of the survey to the "identifying land features" concept.

Assessment Evaluations (Real-time)

  • Above Expectation
    • case: when the learner receives an Expert score on the survey
  • At Expectation
    • case: when the learner receives a Journeyman score on the survey
  • Below Expectation
    • case: when the learner receives a Novice score on the survey
  • Unknown
    • case: the default value, if the learner doesn't receive a score on the survey

Possible Scored Evaluations (overall)

  • counts - the number of times the survey score is Expert or Journeyman.
  • provides the amount of time from when the condition started being assessed to the time when the learner received an Expert or Journeyman score on the survey.

Miscellaneous details

  • the condition completes when the survey score is Expert or Journeyman
  • the condition can be repeated

Intended Training Applications and Domains

  • not associated with a Training Application because this is tied to surveys given by GIFT

Consumed State Messages (used for assessment)

  • none

GIFT class: mil.arl.gift.domain.knowledge.condition.SimpleSurveyAssessmentCondition
