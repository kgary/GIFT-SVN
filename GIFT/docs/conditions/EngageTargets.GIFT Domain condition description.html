Engage Targets Condition

This condition assesses whether one or more team members are properly engaging targets. To properly engage targets the team members specified must fire at targets that enter the member's weapon cone.

The author can define the targets, time to engage, field of view and weapon cone as inputs to the assessment algorithm.  Only targets that are healthy will be considered for assessment.  This prevents being assessed against objects that are dead/incapacitated.

The goal of this condition is to not assess every possible situation.  Hopefully it provides an initial assessment result that can be used by observers/trainers to make additional assessments or over-rides.

Assessment Evaluations (Real-time)

  • Above Expectation
    • case: assessed team member(s) fired at designated target(s) while that target was in the weapon cone within the time limit selected for Above Expectation. 
    • case: the average assessment of all assessed team member(s) firing at designated target(s) while that target was in the weapon cone in this instance averages to Above Expectation.
  • At Expectation
    • case: assessed team member(s) fired at designated target while that target was in the weapon cone within the time limit selected for At Expectation.
    • case: the average assessment of all assessed team member(s) firing at designated target(s) while that target was in the weapon cone in this instance averages to At Expectation.
  • Below Expectation
    • case: assessed team member(s) did NOT fire at designated target while target was in the weapon conde within the time limit selected for At Expectation.  Note that Below Expectation will be assessed whether or not the assessed team member fires after the time limit for At Expectation expires.
    • case: the average assessment of all assessed team member(s) firing at designated target(s) while the target was in the weapon conde in this instance averages to Below Expectation.
  • Unknown
    • case: The default initial value of this condition.

Possible Scored Evaluations (overall)

  • counts the number of violations (going from At to Below Expecation)
  • sums the duration of time in Below Expectation

Miscellaneous details

  • this condition does not complete
  •  If the assessed team member moves the weapon cone away from the designated target without firing before the time limit for at expectation expires, no assessment occurs.

Intended Training Applications and Domains

  • any application that can produce entity state and weapon fire messages

Consumed State Messages (used for assessment)

  • GIFT Entity State
  • GIFT Weapon Fire
