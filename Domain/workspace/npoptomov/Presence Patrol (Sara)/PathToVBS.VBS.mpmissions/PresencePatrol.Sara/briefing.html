Mission: Your Mission is to conduct a Presence Patrol in the village of Ortego with the intent to observe suspected insurgent activity.

Execution: You will begin at the northern end of a suspected insurgent safe house compound moving South along the West side of compound to a road, then proceed North along the East side of compound to the Northeast corner where a HMMWV is waiting for mission complete.

• Rules of Engagement (ROE) are in effect - Do not fire your weapon unless fired upon

• Observe only DO NOT ENTER compound

• Clear all Danger Areas and Corners

• Use walls for cover but maintain distance (Do not silhouette yourself above fence, crouch when needed)

• Complete patrol in 10 minutes or less
